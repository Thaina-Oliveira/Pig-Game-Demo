On a turn, a player rolls the die repeatedly. The goal is to accumulate as many points as possible, adding up the numbers rolled on the dice. When you press the gold button, you will add the acumula points to your preview points and the game will turn to next player. However, if a player rolls a 1, the player's turn is over and any points they have accumulated during this turn are forfeited.
